Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Ingantt different from other project management apps?

Ingantt combines powerful AI-driven project planning with an intuitive mobile interface, making it perfect for managing projects on the go. Our focus on personal project management means you get all the features you need without the complexity of team collaboration tools.

Do I need an internet connection to use Ingantt?

No, Ingantt works offline. You only need an internet connection when syncing with Google Drive or using AI features.

Can I try Ingantt before buying?

While we don't offer a free trial, you can see detailed feature previews and tutorials on our Google Play store page. Plus, Google Play offers a refund window if the app doesn't meet your needs.

What happens to my data if I uninstall the app?

Your project files remain safely stored in your Google Drive even after uninstalling. You can always reinstall the app and access your projects, or export them to various formats for backup.
