07Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ingantt for Android?

Ingantt for Android is a professional project management tool that runs natively on your Android device. It allows you to create and manage project plans on the go, with AI-assisted planning and MS Project compatibility.

Can I import my existing Microsoft Project files?

Yes! Ingantt fully supports importing .MPP and .XML files from Microsoft Project. Your project data, including tasks, resources, and dependencies, will be preserved during the import process.

How does the AI project generation work?

Our AI assistant can generate complete project plans from simple text descriptions. Just describe your project goals and requirements, and the AI will create a structured plan with tasks, timelines, and dependencies based on best practices.

Is there a free trial for the Android version?

No. The Android version is a one-time purchase, with no subscription or free trial.

How can I purchase Ingantt for Android?

Simply visit the Google Play Store on your Android device and purchase it there. You will own the app perpetually with no recurring charges.
